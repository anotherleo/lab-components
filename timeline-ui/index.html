Timeline

Uma jornada de Excelência e Inovação

Veja os principais momentos da história da Fusion Events.

1993 Birth of Fusion Events - A passionate group of event enthusiasts come together to form Fusion Events, driven by their shared love for creating unforgettable experiences.
2001
The Breakthrough - Fusion Events gains recognition for their innovative approach to event planning, earning their first major industry award and establishing themselves as a rising star in the field.
2008
The Grand Expansion - Fusion Events expands its operations internationally, organizing and managing large-scale events in multiple countries, solidifying their position as a global leader in the event industry.
2015
A Technological Leap - Recognizing the power of technology in transforming the event experience, Fusion Events invests heavily in cutting-edge event management software and digital platforms, revolutionizing their approach and enhancing attendee engagement.
2020
Pivoting in a Pandemic - As the world faces the challenges of a global pandemic, Fusion Events quickly adapts, shifting focus to virtual and hybrid events, leading the way in redefining the industry and ensuring that the magic of events continues to thrive, even in uncertain times.
2023
Celebrating 30 Years - Fusion Events reaches a milestone, marking 30 years of creating extraordinary moments and memorable celebrations. With a portfolio of successful events spanning the globe, they continue to push boundaries, embracing new technologies and trends to craft unparalleled experiences for their clients and attendees.
